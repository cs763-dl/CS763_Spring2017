    Topics to be covered (tentative list)

    • The data driven paradigm, feed forwards networks, back propagation and chain rule
    • CNNs and their building blocks - ReLU, MaxPool, Convolution, MSECriterion, CrossEntropy and SoftMax
    • Vanishing gradients, residual blocks, visualizing and understanding CNNs
    • CNN applications, CNN compression
    • Siamese and triplet networks, (and if time permits) generative models: deep dream, neural art and GANs
